## Recomendacao por Fase

### Pre-MVP (Validacao)
- **Landing**: Carrd ou Framer ($0-20)
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics + Hotjar free
- **Email**: Mailerlite free
- **Design**: Figma free + Canva free
- **Forms**: Google Forms ou Formspree
- **Total**: $0-20/mes

### MVP (Construcao)
- **Stack**: Next.js + Supabase + Vercel ($0-25)
- **Analytics**: PostHog free
- **Pagamento**: Stripe ou Pagar.me
- **Email**: Postmark ($10)
- **Projeto**: Linear free
- **Monitoring**: Sentry free + UptimeRobot free
- **Total**: $10-60/mes

### Growth (Escala)
- **Analytics**: PostHog/Mixpanel + ChartMogul
- **CRM**: HubSpot ou Intercom
- **Automacao**: N8N ou Zapier
- **Email**: ConvertKit ou Klaviyo
- **Total**: $100-500/mes

### Series A+ (Enterprise)
- Upgrade para paid tiers de tudo
- Datadog, Amplitude, Zendesk enterprise
- **Total**: $1K-10K/mes
